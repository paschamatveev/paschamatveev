Hello! My name is Pascha and I am currently a student at the University of Washington where I study Neuroscience and Neural Computation and Engineering. I am interested in learning and memory and want to explore more molecular approaches to compliment my systems, behavioral, and computational knowledge.

Other than my research, I am an avid community member and love to host and participate in outreach programs and teaching efforts.

I hope to enter graduate school in fall of 2025 (interview season is upon us!). Check back to see where I end up.
